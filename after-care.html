Nervous?

  • Do not consume alcohol in the 24 hour period before your tattoo appointment.
  • Please eat something before you come and bring a snack if you're sitting for more than a couple of hours to keep your strength up.
  • Getting up close and personal, so take a shower and be un time!
  • Visit the bank machine and have your money and I.D. ready.
  • Wear clothing that is appropriate but remember tattoo ink is hard to get out of clothes
  • Feel free to bring moral support but remember you only have two hands to hold!

Healing

  • Let your tattoo dry for 2 days, then apply recommended aftercare product 2-3 times daily.
  • Avoid swimming pools, hot tubs and direct sunlight for a minimum of three weeks.
  • For temporary relief of swelling take Ibuprofen or any other over the counter anti-inflammatory.
  • Wash your tattoo daily with unscented soaps.
  • Do not pick your tattoo! let it heal naturally!
